Oatmeal

Oatmeal is a simple, wholesome, and nutritious breakfast that provides lasting energy. Made with creamy oats and topped with fresh fruits, a drizzle of honey, and a sprinkle of nuts or seeds, it’s both delicious and satisfying.

This versatile dish can be customized with various toppings like banana slices, berries, cinnamon, or even peanut butter for extra flavor. Whether enjoyed warm on a cold morning or as an overnight chilled version, oatmeal is a quick and healthy way to start your day! 🥣

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (240ml) milk or water
  • ½ cup (45g) rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • Toppings: fresh berries, banana slices, nuts, or seeds

Instructions:

  1. Heat the liquid (milk or water) in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the oats and stir occasionally, cooking for about 5 minutes until creamy.
  3. Stir in honey and cinnamon (if using) and mix well.
  4. Serve warm with your favorite toppings like berries, banana, or nuts.

Enjoy your healthy and delicious oatmeal! 🍓🍌
